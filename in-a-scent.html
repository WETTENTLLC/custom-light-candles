Antoine’s In-A-Scent (Innoscent): A Signature Fragrance Experience

The Essence of Luxury and Personalization

Introducing Antoine’s In-A-Scent (stylized as Innoscent), a bespoke fragrance collection that redefines the art of scent. Created by Master Candle Maker Antoine Montgomery, this exclusive line blends the finest essential oils, premium waxes, and expert craftsmanship to deliver a truly immersive sensory experience. More than just candles, Innoscent represents a journey into self-expression, relaxation, and refined luxury.

What Makes In-A-Scent (Innoscent) Special?

Unlike mass-produced candles that offer generic scents, In-A-Scent is designed to be deeply personal and uniquely tailored to each customer’s preferences. Every candle in the collection is handcrafted by Antoine himself, ensuring the highest level of quality and attention to detail. From carefully selected ingredients to precise scent blending, every aspect of Innoscent is a reflection of Antoine’s dedication to excellence.

Handcrafted Scents for Every Mood and Occasion

Each Innoscent candle is meticulously formulated to create an atmosphere that aligns with your emotions, enhances your environment, and complements your lifestyle. Whether you seek tranquility, motivation, warmth, or elegance, Antoine’s expertly curated fragrance combinations offer something for every occasion.

Signature Fragrance Profiles:

  • Serenity: A soothing blend of lavender, chamomile, and vanilla, perfect for unwinding after a long day.
  • Enlightenment: A bright fusion of citrus zest, white tea, and jasmine, designed to invigorate the senses.
  • Harmony: Earthy sandalwood, amber, and musk, providing grounding energy and balance.
  • Passion: A sensual mix of exotic spices, rich florals, and deep musk for a luxurious ambiance.
  • Revival: Crisp eucalyptus, mint, and rosemary to refresh and awaken the spirit.

Customized Creations – Crafted Just for You

At Innoscent, we believe that fragrance should be an extension of your personality. That’s why Antoine offers **custom scent development**, allowing you to create a fragrance that is uniquely yours. Whether you want a candle that reminds you of a cherished memory, a signature scent for your home, or an exclusive fragrance for your business, Antoine will work with you to craft a blend that embodies your vision.

Premium Ingredients for a Superior Experience

Antoine’s dedication to quality means that every In-A-Scent candle is made with:

  • High-quality soy, beeswax, and coconut wax blends for a clean, long-lasting burn.
  • Lead-free, eco-friendly wicks to ensure a safe and even flame.
  • Pure essential oils and phthalate-free fragrance oils for a luxurious scent throw without harmful chemicals.
  • Hand-poured, small-batch craftsmanship to maintain consistency and excellence in every candle.
