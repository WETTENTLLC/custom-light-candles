Our Candle-Making Process

From premium ingredients to hand-poured perfection, every step ensures exceptional quality.

Step 1: Premium Wax Selection

We carefully select the finest soy, beeswax, and coconut wax blends for clean, long-lasting burns.

Step 2: Fragrance Blending

Our master candle maker Antoine personally blends each fragrance to create unique, captivating scents.

Step 3: Wick Preparation

Premium cotton wicks are carefully selected and prepared to ensure optimal burn performance.

Step 4: Hand Pouring

Each candle is hand-poured with precision, ensuring perfect consistency and quality in every piece.

Step 5: Curing Process

Candles are allowed to cure for optimal scent throw and burn quality before packaging.

Step 6: Quality Control

Every candle undergoes rigorous quality testing to meet our exceptional standards before shipping.
