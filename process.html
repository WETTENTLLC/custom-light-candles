The Candle-Making Process

Step 1: Selecting the Ingredients

Carefully choose high-quality wax, wicks, and fragrances.

Step 2: Melting the Wax

Heat the wax to the perfect temperature for blending.

Step 3: Adding the Fragrance

Blend premium fragrances to create the perfect aroma.

Step 4: Pouring the Wax

Carefully pour the wax into custom molds or jars.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

Trim the wicks and package the candles beautifully.
